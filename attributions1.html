Lab Team

Throughout the season our wet lab team spent days and nights in the lab. Maksym Zaradniuk was the responsible student who managed the process from the team members site. The process was guided by more experienced iGEMers, as well as instructors and advisors.

Modeling team

Modeling team did the research, built, and studied the behavior of the models. The models aimed to predict and maximize the yields of products of bioproduction.

Public engagement

Public engagement team is a very diverse one, its general responsibility is to convey to the world the information about our project, our team, and the science field itself. This means taking and posting pictures and videos, participating in and organizing the workshops, fairs, exhibitions, and other interesting activities. But also some very special tasks were completed under the hood of the PE department such as writing and recording a team song (Kate and Amina), creating the comics (Glib and Dima), and designing an escape room (Norin and Avishan).

Collaborations team

Collaborations team managed inner-iGEM communications: they created a team postcard, took part, and initiated their very own partnerships with different teams worldwide. Also, this group conducted the desired surveys and was very intensively involved in the life of the team's social media. Under their jurisdiction, even a story for Bob's Lelogy cell adventure was written by Norin.

Integrated Human practices

The Integrated HP team conducted thorough market research in order to investigate the potential impact our end product could have on the pharma, food, and cosmetic industries. The team also got in contact with several stakeholders in the field, including professors, researchers, and company managers to validate our findings about the feasibility of our product in the market. Also thanks to the IHP group, our team got in touch with the professor who helped us to produce the nanoparticles.

Fundraising

Fundraising team was working on writing a proposal letter, studying, and contacting potential sponsors.

Wiki team

Wiki team was responsible for creating a team's design code (Frida), constructing and maintaining the whole wiki page (creating templates, their realization, filling, and formalization of pages with content). These guys are the gods of the coding, kings of sleepless nights, and lords of the coffee machine.
